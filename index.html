ABOUT

MS-06S Char's Zaku II

This Gundam Plastic Model is the iconic MS-06S Char's Zaku II, a mobile suit featured in the Mobile Suit Gundam: The Origin manga and its OVA adaptation. It was piloted by Char Aznable during the One Year War who is the rival of Amuro, who piloted the popular RX-78-02 Gundam.
